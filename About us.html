FunZone is an upscale, family entertainment center featuring roller/inline skating, a huge indoor multi-level soft playground, exciting video arcade, food cafe, and prize redemption center/proshop. Funzone (formerly known as Wooddale Recreation Center) was built in 1974 by Russell Johnston. It was originally designed as a multi-purpose family activity center offering indoor/outdoor tennis as well as indoor rollerskating. In 1978, we moved the tennis into a new building which was constructed next door, sharing a common lobby. In 2006, The facility underwent a major renovation both indoor and outdoor. The tennis club officially became Wooddale Tennis & Health. The skating center, with the addition of the massive playground was named Wooddale Fun Zone. Fun Zone/Wooddale Tennis & Health is still family owned /operated. Our Mission statement is.....to provide a clean, safe, environment for people of all ages to enjoy wholesome, healthy, FUN!!!!
